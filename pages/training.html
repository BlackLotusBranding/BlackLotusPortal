Lesson 1 — Welcome to Black Lotus

A Scout represents Black Lotus Branding in public. Your role is to help the company grow by noticing businesses that may need branding, graphics, websites, marketing support, or stronger online presentation.

  • Be respectful and professional.
  • Never pressure people.
  • Protect company and client information.
  • Use the Daily Work Log so management can understand your activity.

Lesson 2 — What Scouts Actually Do

Scouts are not expected to design graphics or close complicated deals. Your main job is to find possible leads, record them clearly, and start respectful conversations when appropriate.

Good Scout tasks
Find businesses, collect links, write notes, send soft introductions, update logs.
Not your job
Promise prices, argue online, make contracts, pressure customers, or share confidential files.

Lesson 3 — Finding Leads

A lead is a person or business that may benefit from Black Lotus Branding services. Good leads often show signs that their brand needs help.

  • Facebook business pages with outdated graphics.
  • Businesses without a clean logo.
  • Small businesses with no website or weak link-in-bio.
  • Local service providers who rely heavily on word of mouth.
  • Businesses posting often but without consistent branding.

Places to look: Facebook groups, Facebook pages, Instagram, TikTok, Google Maps, local community pages, flea markets, events, lawn care groups, beauty pages, food vendors, mechanics, contractors, artists, and local service providers.

Hi! I came across your page and really like what you're building. I work with Black Lotus Branding, and we help small businesses with graphics, branding, websites, and marketing materials. If you ever want help polishing your business look, we'd love to connect.

Lesson 4 — Using the Daily Work Log

The Daily Work Log is how Scouts show their work. It helps management see who you found, what action was taken, and what needs follow-up.

  • Record the business/person name.
  • Add the link to their page or profile.
  • Write what you noticed.
  • Mark whether you contacted them.
  • Add follow-up notes if needed.

Lesson 5 — Google Workspace Basics

Scouts should become comfortable opening and using the Black Lotus Google tools.

  • Google Drive: find files and resources.
  • Google Calendar: check meetings and important dates.
  • Google Meet: join training or team calls.
  • Google Forms: submit official requests and reports.
  • Google Sheets: update your Daily Work Log.

Graduation — Ready to Scout

You are ready to begin once you understand the mission, know how to find leads, can update the Daily Work Log, and can access Google Workspace tools.
